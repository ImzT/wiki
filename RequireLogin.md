#RequireLogin

[[_TOC_]]

##About

Gets or sets whether users are required to go through the login page specified by the [[LoginUrl|/API/CodeSamples/LoginUrl]] setting before accessing the reporting application. A user will be considered logged in when the [[CurrentUserName|/API/CodeSamples/CurrentUserName]] setting has a value other than the default.

**Default Value:** False
